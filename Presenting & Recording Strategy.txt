Presenting & Recording Strategy
1. Connect with a monitor using the HDMI cable;
2. Hit F5 to start the PPT;
3. Swap the view to the mmonitor's;
4. Open EVPlayer for cording;
5. Set the proper configuration for the EVPlayer for the best quality of videos to be recorded;
6. Configure the connected camera view (size&location) making the opened-PPT view as a background;
7. Start recording.
